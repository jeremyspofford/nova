Mockup 04 — Singularity

What it is

  • A pure presence view — no nodes, no graph. Just Nova's state of mind, ambient and legible from across the room.
  • Drag to orbit — tilt from near edge-on to top-down; scroll to zoom.
  • Idle — the accretion disk swirls slowly. Memory at rest.
  • Retrieving — the disk accelerates and amber streaks of context fall over the horizon: Nova pulling memories in.
  • Responding — the event horizon pulses, a slow heartbeat of light while the answer streams.

Where it could live

  • The Brain page's third lens next to Galaxy and Orrery.
  • Or smaller: the Chat page's thinking indicator, a dashboard idle screen, the onboarding hero.

Open questions for you

  • This view leans into violet/pink beyond the teal+amber system — keep the wider palette as a Brain-only exception, or re-tint to strict Nova colors?
  • Is presence-without-information enough to be a Brain lens, or is this really the chat/loading identity?
  • Should retrieval hits flash a count on the ring (“6 memories”) so it stays informative?

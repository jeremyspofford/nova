Mockup 02 — Synapse

What's different from 01 (Galaxy)

  • Dendrites, not wires — links are curved and sway slightly, like tissue.
  • Signals travel — retrieval fires action potentials: bright pulses race along the links, hop by hop, and each arrival makes the target neuron fire (expanding ring).
  • Idle is alive — spontaneous micro-firings ripple the network even when nothing is asked, like background brain activity.
  • Responding pulls inward: pulses stream toward the retrieved cluster while it breathes — the network “concentrating.”
